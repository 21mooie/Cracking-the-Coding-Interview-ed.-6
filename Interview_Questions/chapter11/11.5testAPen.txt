Test a Pen: How would you test a pen?

Who will this pen be used by? 
    Is it anyone? Is it children? Is it a certain kind of professional?
What are use cases? 
    Is this pen meant to be used only on paper? Should it be able to write well on leather?
    What kind of ink should it produce? Should the ink be dried by a certain time etc.
What are bound of use?
    How long should the ink be good for? Should the ink bleed through paper or not? How smudge
    resistent should it be? How about the feel? How should it feel in your hand?
What are the stress/failure situations?
    Is it a failure if the pen doesn't work after being in the freezer?
    Should the pen be able to withstand being sat on? Under no circumstances should the pen burst?
